Title: 六角館さくら堂KYOTO (Rokkakukan Sakurado KYOTO) Official Site
Description: This site is the official online presence of Rokkakukan Sakurado KYOTO, a specialty store in Kyoto offering high-quality makeup brushes and related accessories. Located in a renovated traditional Kyoto machiya, the shop features over 150 types of brushes crafted using the renowned Kumano technique from Hiroshima. Products include cheek brushes, concealer brushes, and silk eyelash curl-up brushes, among others. The store also offers kimono rentals and brush care accessories. Visitors can enjoy a traditional Kyoto shopping experience with a modern touch.
